Understanding columns

The width of the screen is divided into 12 units. On a wide screen 4 columns are shown, reducing to 3, 2 and finally 1 column at the smallest screen width.

The syntax to acheve this is col-'screen size'-'Number of units': col-lg-3 col-md-4 col-sm-6 col-12

View the page at different widths to observe columns changing.
